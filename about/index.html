about me

Born and raised in the suburbs of Milwaukee, Wisconsin, I recently graduated from Wesleyan University ('15) with a Bachelor of Arts in computer science and science in society. In 2013 I studied computer science and entrepreneurship in Budapest, Hungary at the Aquincum Institute of Technology. I am currently a part of Apple's Maps team.

Though I find great satisfaction in creating software that makes everyday tasks simpler and more enjoyable, I also care greatly about how that software functions in a user's (read: non-developer's) hands. From this, two things follow: First, I am fixated on the design of the things I create – not just how it looks, but how it is used. Second, and this is where the science in society major originates, I am also very intersted in how new technologies change the way people live and act – socially, intellectually, emotionally, etc. The technology that we use on a daily basis has changed at a nearly unfathomable scale in the past few decades, and I am interested in studying how that impacts us individually as well as from a broader societal perspective.


housing In the Bay (want to be roommates?)

Wesleyan requires students to live on campus, so while I do not have former landlords, I am happy to provide former roommate references. I lived in San Francisco in 2014 and Palo Alto in 2013 for summer internships, so I know the Bay well.

I try to keep a consistent schedule during the week, leaving around 7:00 and returning at 19:00 or so. I keep my things organized, and generally work to keep my living spaces clean. I cook somewhat regularly (2-3 times a week), but always clean up immediately afterward. I usually listen to music when working, but will switch to headphones if I want things loud. On weekends, I generally sleep in and enjoy going out with friends at night.

My ideal roommate/s is/are conversational and respectful of shared spaces. I am queer, and would prefer to live with another queer-identifying person if possible. Beyond that, I am flexible and would just like to have another person around!

If this sounds good to you, please shoot me an email! I would love to chat :)
