About me

Born and raised in the suburbs of Milwaukee, I am now studying computer science and science in society at Wesleyan University. For the fall semester of 2013, I will be studying abroad in Budapest Hungary at AIT. Here, I am taking courses in computer science as well as entrepreneurship.

Though I find great satisfaction in creating software that makes everyday tasks simpler and more enjoyable, I also care greatly about how that software functions in a user's (read: non-developer's) hands. From this, I have chosen my second major, science in society with a concentration in sociology. Through this I am studying the impacts technology has and does have on societies around the world.


Technical Things for Geeks

I love geeks – It means you have passion for something. When I grow up, I want to be a geek.

Before this website, I had almost no experience with HTML or CSS, and equally limited experience with javascript on static webpages. As such this site is a rough start, though development thus far has been very smooth provided I have a large enough chunk of time to devote. The code was written in vim by only myself (hence the externally hosted photo collection and blog) save for google analytics code. As a implement these features myself, I will update the site to show more of my work. All code to date is viewable on my github.

My main text editor is vim, though I started with emacs in COMP 211 at Wesleyan and have recently been playing with sublime text 2. I mainly work in python and Standard ML/NJ for academic work, though I have experience with Javascript, Ruby, and a few others (see my resume).
